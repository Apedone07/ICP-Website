Innovation Pathway Program

Description Of Our Program

The Innovation Career Pathways was made to help students in Worcester Public Schools explore different careers through technical education. In the program, students pick a career they like, get recognized credentials, plan for college and careers, take college-level classes for career growth, and boost their experience with an internship or project.

Worcester Public Schools teamed up with many organizations and businesses to create a program that fits students' needs and the work market. We're proud to work with:

    MassHire Central Region Workforce Board & One8 Foundation & Worcester Regional Chamber of Commerce

Our Program includes:

Career And Academic Planning - this class will provide support, college and career planning, industry connections, technology knowledge and skills, employability skills, and financial skills

2 Technical Classes

From November to mid-March, Classes are held on Tuesdays and Thursdays (from 2:30 to 5:15 at Worcester Technical High School). Transportation to Worcester tech is available, with early dismissal at 1:00 from school. Students can use bus passes or ask for parent or guardian pickup for the trip home. If classes are canceled due to snow or other reasons, makeup sessions take place the following week. Students earn credit on their transcript for these classes.

100 hours of paid Internship or Capstone opportunities

In which students will receive WPS credit on their transcript and opportunities for internship during the Spring, Summer, or Fall
