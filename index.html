ICP

  • The Innovation Career Pathways was made to help students in Worcester Public Schools explore different careers through technical education. In the program, students pick a career they like, get recognized credentials, plan for college and careers, take college-level classes for career growth, and boost their experience with a summer internship or project.
  • Worcester Public Schools teamed up with organizations and businesses to create a program that fits students' needs and the job market. We're proud to work with:
    • MassHire Central Region Workforce Board
    • One8 Foundation
    • Worcester Regional Chamber of Commerce

Our Program Also includes:

  • Career And Academic Planning - this class will provide support, college and career planning, industry connections, technology knowledge and skills, employability skills, and financial skills

2 Technical Classes

  • From November to mid-March, Classes are held on Tuesday and Thursday (from 1:30 to 5:15 at Worcester Technical High School. Transportation to Worcester tech is available, with early dismissal at 1:00 from school. Students can use WRTA passes or arrange for guardian pickup for the trip home. If classes are canceled due to snow or other reasons, makeup sessions take place the following week. Students earn WPS credit on their transcript for these classes.
