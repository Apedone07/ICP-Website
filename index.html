ICP

The Innovation Career Pathways was made to help students in Worcester Public Schools explore different careers through technical education. In the program, students pick a career they like, get recognized credentials, plan for college and careers, take college-level classes for career growth, and boost their experience with a summer internship or project.

Worcester Public Schools teamed up with organizations and businesses to create a program that fits students' needs and the job market. We're proud to work with:
MassHire Central Region Workforce Board One8 Foundation Worcester Regional Chamber of Commerce
